Jaeyuk Bokkeum (Spicy Pork Bulgogi)

This is a delicious korean recipe made by chef Jia Choi. It is spicy, sweet, salty and delicious. It is best accompanied with a steaming bowl of rice and it is very easy to make. This recipe makes 4 servings.

Ingredients

  • 500g of pork, sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1tbsp of ginger, minced
  • 2tbsp of gochujang
  • 2tbsp of soy sauce
  • 1tbsp of toasted sesame oil
  • 2tbsp of mirin
  • 1 or 2 tbsp of gochugaru (optional, but flavorful and spicy)
  • 1tbsp of black pepper
  • 1/2tbsp of brown sugar
  • 1 handful of spring onions, finely chopped (reserve white part for sauce and green part for garnishing)
  • 1 head of broccoli (optional, but nutritional and tasty)
  • 4 medium sized carrots (also optional, but nutritional and tasty)
  • 1tbsp of oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the sauce ahead of time by adding the gochujang, soy sauce, mirin, sesame oil, garlic, ginger, spring onion, gochugaru, black pepper and sugar into a container. Let it develop its flavors for at least half an hour in the fridge.
  2. Add the oil to a hot pan or pot, and add the pork. Cook until it's brown and crispy.
  3. Remove the pork, add or remove oil as needed, and add the veggies. Cook until they are slightly soft.
  4. Add the meat back to the pot and add the sauce. Stir and adjust seasoning as needed. Simmer for 3 minutes or until the sauce is hot and incorporated into the meat and veggies.
  5. Serve with rice and enjoy!
