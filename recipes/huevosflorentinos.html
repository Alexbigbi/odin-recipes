Huevos Florentinos

This breakfast egg recipe is easy, cheap, nutritious and very easy to make. It doesn't compromise flavor for nutrition, and it's an excellent way to start your morning.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbsp of olive oil
  • 1 handful of spinach, rinsed
  • 1 or 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Goat cheese
  • Your bread of preference (white bread, english muffins, sliced baguette, ciabatta, etc.)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Mince the garlic and set to the side.
  2. Add the oil to a hot pan and add the garlic, stirring constantly to not burn it.
  3. Once it gets a little golden brown, add the washed spinach to the pan. Add water if needed and add the salt and pepper.
  4. Cook until wilted.
  5. Remove the garlic and spinach from the pan. Add more oil if needed, then add the eggs to the pan. Add seasoning and cook to your desired doneness.
  6. Toast your bread of preference and spread the goat cheese on top of it. Top it with the spinach and garlic, then the eggs.
  7. Enjoy!
